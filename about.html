About

Hi! My name is Hanlin Wang and I'm currently a senior at Dartmouth College pursuing a degree in film studies, digital arts, and computer science. Working as a cinematographer, editor, and photographer, both professionally and informally, has shaped my background as a visual storyteller. My goal moving forward is to explore the intersection of technology and visual entertainment as it manifests in the animation, VFX, and VR/AR industries.
In my free time I enjoy jamming on the violin, finding new music to listen to, and catching up on my ever-growing movie watchlist.
You can reach me at allegrohanlin@gmail.com to connect, chat, or ask about my resume.
